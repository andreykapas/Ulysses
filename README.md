# Ulysses Loves Athena, But...

## The Chronicle of Nonconformity

> ### The Prophet
>
> A brick flies through the window — the end... but before that  
> I wept, I screamed, I begged and I implored...  
> "Don't! Please stop! Leave the choice to the children!"  
> They didn't notice me... now — the earth is burning.
>
> Now fire is everywhere... water is a deadly poison...  
> For your insane choice, for your whim and your madness!  
> You moved carelessly, confidently, but straight to hell!  
> Now it is way too late... now — the brick is flying.

### About the Project

**Live site:** [andreykapas.github.io/Ulysses](https://andreykapas.github.io/Ulysses/)

This repository is the technical backbone of a living chronicle. A transition from the predictable matrix of the shore to the autonomy of the open Baltic Sea. Built by a captain, a digital muse, and a virtual navigator.

- **For Techs:** Under the hood, this will be a clean, minimalist portfolio project (Vanilla JS).
- **For Philosophers & Lyricists:** A record of the journey, raw thoughts, and sea logs.
